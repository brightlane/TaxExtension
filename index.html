What Is a Tax Extension?

A tax extension gives you more time to file your tax return, but not to pay any tax you owe. Late payment can still create penalties and interest.

An online service like TaxExtension.com lets you file a tax extension online by submitting Form 4868 directly to the IRS with electronic approval.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a tax extension do?

A tax extension gives you up to 6 extra months to file your federal return, but does not extend the payment deadline.

Does a tax extension extend the payment deadline?

No. A tax extension only extends the filing deadline, not the payment deadline.

Can I file an extension if I owe money?

Yes. You can file an extension even if you expect to owe, but pay your estimated tax on time to avoid penalties.
